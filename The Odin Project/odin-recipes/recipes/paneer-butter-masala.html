Paneer Butter Masala


Description

Paneer Butter Masala is a rich, creamy North Indian curry made with soft paneer cubes simmered in a smooth tomato-butter gravy. Lightly spiced and mildly sweet, it’s best enjoyed with naan or jeera rice.

Ingredients

  • Paneer (cubed)
  • Butter, oil
  • Tomatoes (pureed)
  • Onion (paste)
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Cashew paste or cream
  • Red chilli powder, garam masala
  • Salt, sugar
  • Fresh cream

Steps

  1. Heat butter and a little oil in a pan on medium flame. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until the raw aroma disappears.
  2. Add onion paste and cook, stirring continuously, until it turns light golden and the mixture becomes thick.
  3. Pour in the tomato puree and cook well until the masala thickens and butter starts separating from the sides.
  4. Add red chilli powder, garam masala, salt, and a pinch of sugar. Mix thoroughly and cook for another minute.
  5. Stir in cashew paste or fresh cream to make the gravy rich and smooth.
  6. Add paneer cubes and a little water or milk to adjust consistency. Gently mix.
  7. Simmer for 5–7 minutes on low heat so the paneer absorbs the flavors.
  8. Finish with fresh cream and butter on top. Serve hot with naan, roti, or jeera rice.
